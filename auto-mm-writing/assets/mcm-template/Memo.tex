\section{Non-technical Memo}
\label{sec:memo}

% PLACEHOLDER — auto-mm-writing fills this from the problem's memo audience
% (e.g. "to the city council", "to the show's producers"). Keep technical
% jargon out; lead with recommendations.

To: \texttt{<recipient role from problem statement>} \\
From: Team \texttt{<control number>} \\
Re:   \texttt{<one-line subject>}

\medskip

Opening: one sentence on what this memo addresses.

\medskip

Findings: bulleted list of the 2-3 most relevant findings in plain language.
Avoid equations; use percentages, dollar amounts, time ranges instead.

\medskip

Recommendations: concrete actions the recipient can take. Numbered list with
one-sentence justifications.

\medskip

Closing: one sentence acknowledging limitations or next steps.
